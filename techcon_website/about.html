Our History

Since its inception in 2005, TechCon has grown from a small developer meetup into one of the world's premier tech conferences. Over the years, it has hosted thousands of professionals, entrepreneurs, and students, fostering innovation and collaboration.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to empower the global tech community by sharing knowledge, nurturing talent, and inspiring the next generation of innovators. We believe in the power of open collaboration and disruptive thinking to shape the future of technology.

  • Susan Wojcicki – Former YouTube CEO. Spoke on digital media evolution and creator economy.

  • Satya Nadella – CEO of Microsoft. Delivered a keynote on cloud, data, and empathy-driven leadership.
